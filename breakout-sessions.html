Breakout sessions

What is a breakout session?

A breakout session is a workshop, presentation or discussion on a particular topic.

Leading a breakout session

Leading a breakout session at CodeFest is simple:

  1. Create a one-slide presentation of the topic of your breakout session (example of a one-slide presentation for a breakout session).
  2. Present your breakout session to CodeFest attendees.
  3. Gather the participants and run your breakout session.
