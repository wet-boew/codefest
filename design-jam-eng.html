Design jam

A design jam is a collaborative design session in which teams of participants will work on user experience problems. Teams will be formed on-site to address a variety of areas of interest including branding, information architecture, content, flow, and personas using a toolkit of UX activities.

Each team will have the opportunity to share their findings and ideas with the rest of the Jam, and even collaborate on elements of common interest. The goal is to come up with ideas and a plan to improve how we communicate WET to a number of different audiences, how to organize all the WET information and documentation, and to suggest the overall user experience (including the feel, or branding) of WET and its online components.

Don't worry if you're new to all this, mentors will be on-site to help guide you through the process: checking in, offering suggestions, asking questions, explaining techniques, etc. The Jam is a great way for developers, designers, communicators and UX practitioners to collaborate in real-time, without needing to know a single line of code.
