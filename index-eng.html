Web Experience Toolkit Codefest 2.0

The Web Experience Toolkit community is hosting WxT Codefest 2.0. The event will take place August 8th and 9th, 2013 at the University of Ottawa. As part of Coodefest 2.0 the community is excited to host various regional satellite events and a design jam. It is an open event for public servants, WET contributors and interested organizations.
