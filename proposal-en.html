Tips on making your session accessible

Presenting at CodeFest this year? Powerpoint slides can be challenging for people with vision disability. Here are few tips that you may want to consider:

  • Text: Choose readable fonts (e.g. Arial) and use large text size.
  • Visual aids: Describe verbally any chart or visual materials you are using.
  • Color contrast: Have sufficient color contrast between the text and background of your slides.
  • Summarize key points: Participants with vision loss may not be able to see information presented so ensure to regularly summarize key points.
